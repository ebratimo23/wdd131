Site Name: MovieMate

The name represents a platform where users can receive personalized movie recommendations based on their individual tastes and preferences.

Optional Domain Availability: moviemate.com

Site Purpose

The site functions as a platform where users can receive personalized movie recommendations based on their individual tastes and preferences.

Scenarios

  • What movies would you recommend for someone who enjoyed "Inception" and "Interstellar"?
  • How can I filter movies by genre, release year, or rating?
  • Where can I find a list of my previously recommended movies?

Color Scheme

The selected colors for the site are:

  • Primary Color: Dark Red (#9b1d20)
  • Secondary Color: Dark Gray (#333333)

Typography

The selected font for the site is"Poppins", sans-serif from google fonts. It will be used in:

  • Headings
  • Body text
